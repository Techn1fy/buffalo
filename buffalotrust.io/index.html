ESCROW SOLUTIONS
Buffalo Trust delivers escrow services for secure holding, management, and conditional release of funds or assets in complex or high-value transactions. Offerings include escrow-based and partial settlement options, enabling incremental payments or milestone-based disbursements to optimize liquidity and reduce risk. Programmatic verification and subledger tracking streamline processes and maintain transactional integrity, making these solutions suitable for both traditional and affiliate-driven agreements.
Digital Asset Escrow
Digital Asset Escrow is a secure platform for managing digital asset transactions on public blockchains. It includes automated onboarding, identity verification, and secure custody features such as multi-factor authentication and end-to-end encryption. The platform supports smart contract-based workflows and is designed to meet the needs of clients operating in tokenized or blockchain-integrated environments.
Bespoke Remittance Solutions
Buffalo Trust offers tailored remittance services for scenarios that require structured fund disbursement. These include disaster relief payments, litigation settlements, and other specialized use cases. The trust handles onboarding, compliance screening, payment execution, and reporting, ensuring that funds are distributed securely and in accordance with regulatory requirements.

Trust Based Services
Buffalo Trust offers a suite of trust-based financial services, including Banking-as-a-Service, Compliance-as-a-Service, and Risk-as-a-Service, in partnership with Innovation Trust Bank International. These services deliver secure, regulated infrastructure for managing accounts, ensuring compliance, and assessing risk in complex financial transactions.
Trust Based Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS)
Buffalo Trust's Trust Based BaaS offering is powered by its institutional relationship with Innovation Trust Bank International. Through this partnership, Buffalo Trust provides integrated financial infrastructure that bridges traditional and digital banking functions. Services include account management, transaction processing, and digital asset supportall delivered through a trust-based framework.
Trust Based Compliance-as-a-Service (CaaS)
Trust Based CaaS is also delivered in partnership with Innovation Bank. It provides a comprehensive compliance infrastructure that includes KYC/KYB onboarding, AML/OFAC screening, and transaction monitoring. Risk-as-a-Service (RaaS)
Buffalo Trust also leverages Risk-as-a-Service through Innovation Bank. RaaS supports credit reviews, financial risk assessments, and mitigation strategies for both tangible and intangible assets. It is particularly useful for counterparties engaged in high-risk or multi-jurisdictional transactions. The service includes access to specialized tools, staff, and systems that align with U.S. regulatory expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is Buffalo Trust?
Buffalo Trust is a Wyoming-based statutory trust that provides escrow, remittance, and trust-based financial infrastructure for complex transactions. It supports both fiat and digital assets and is designed to meet the needs of counterparties operating in regulated and emerging markets.
How is Buffalo Trust different from a traditional escrow provider?
Buffalo Trust combines statutory trust powers with integrated compliance, banking, and risk services. Through its partnership with Innovation Trust Bank International, it delivers Trust Based Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS), Compliance-as-a-Service (CaaS), and Risk-as-a-Service (RaaS), enabling a more secure, scalable, and compliant transaction environment.
Who can use Buffalo Trust?
Buffalo Trust serves high-net-worth individuals, family offices, SMEs, institutional investors, digital asset issuers, program arrangers, and cross-border counterparties. It is particularly well-suited for clients who require structured, multi-party, or jurisdictionally complex financial solutions.
What types of transactions does Buffalo Trust support?
Buffalo Trust supports escrow-based settlements, partial settlements, escrow optimization, digital asset issuance, disaster relief disbursements, litigation settlements, and other bespoke remittance programs.
Is Buffalo Trust regulated?
Yes. Buffalo Trust operates under the Wyoming Statutory Trust Act and partners with Innovation Trust Bank International, a U.S.-supervised financial institution regulated under Puerto Rico's IFE Act No. 273-2012.
Can Buffalo Trust handle crypto and digital assets?
Yes. Buffalo Trust supports both fiat and crypto transactions, including tokenized asset issuance and smart contract-based settlements. It provides secure custody, compliance screening, and programmable disbursement for digital assets.
